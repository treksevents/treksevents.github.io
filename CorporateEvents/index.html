Fashion Forest Corporate CSR Celebri-Tree Day

Venue

Treks Fashion Forest @ The Awana BIO Park
RWG Awana, Genting Highlands

Introduction

This is a Corporate Day in Nature Activity which involves participants to help promote and grow the love for our rainforests via a number of social media applications namely WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and personal blogs.

Free wi-fi is provided at the Fashion Forest to achieve the purpose.

The intended challenge is for each participants either through a selfies or wefies share them with their friends via their choice of social media apps and #Trekscelebri-tree. Treks will as a follow up post the selfies and wefies on TreksS FB, Instagram and blog.

The content of all selfies and wefies must include the person or persons promoting in the background or foreground any plants, animals or forest scenery of interest accompanied with a written promotional comment about the subjects.

Description of Activity

This is a ½ day activity that begins from 9.30am – 1.00pm in the morning or from 2.30pm – 6.00pm in the afternoon.

Activities for the Celebri-Tree Day includes

  1. A Biodiversity Tour of 5 Fashion Forest Galleries
  2. Introduction to Fashion Forest Bar Code Tree ID
  3. Introduction to Fashion Forest Bar Code Quizzes
  4. Introduction to Fashion Forest Biodiversity View Frames
  5. Introduction to Fashion Forest Animated Pergolas
  6. Introduction to Fashion Forest Sculptures
  7. Introduction to Fashion Forest 15 Rattans of Malaysia
  8. Introduction to 10 herbs of the Fashion Forest
  9. Introduction to the Supermodels of the Fashion Forest
  10. Enjoy a 45 minutes Giants of the Fashion Forest Biodiversity Walk
  11. Sign a Celebri-Tree Pledge
  12. Share day’s experience via social media
