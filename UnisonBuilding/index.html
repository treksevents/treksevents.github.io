Unison Building

The Reel Simulator





We help team members to achieve UNITY of mind, vision and effort. We help them UNITE for the collective cause. We help to set up the UNITING process to achieve a UNITED team for the common good.

To achieve the above we create Treks The REEL Simulator movie-themed Unison Building team activities that is abound with self and team-discovery opportunities that stirs the mind-set (that Learns / Charge Up), stimulate thoughts (Unlearn / Discharge) and spurs positive actions (Relearn / Recharge).

To enjoy a great team-day, we offer a measured-paced program with opportunities for members to interact, communicate, and inspire each other and bond for the common cause.

Through empathy, we aim to share the importance of Change (introduce new learning), the importance of continued Divergence (adapting to new ideas / issues / process) and the importance of Survival (progress through ideas / latest effort / strategies / resources).

Through the above we aim to inspire participants to not choose specialization in their immediate field as a means to an end instead, to choose to adjust to new requirements, new heights in order to bridge the gap and reach closer their higher abilities. As obvious in the law of the forest, it is always changing / evolving / improving, it’s about the survival of the fittest (meeting new needs and overcoming new challenges to progress), it is about divergence (if you are not strong, you can depend on others or change).
