Black gibbons & helmeted hornbill

We offer 2 types of special walks for guests

  1. Discover the elusive Black Gibbons
  2. Discover the elusive Helmeted Hornbills

Discover the elusive Black Gibbons

The Black Gibbon also known as the Siamang is in the class of the lesser ape. Gibbons thrive on the abundant fruit trees in their tropical range, and are especially fond of figs. They will occasionally supplement their diet with leaves and insects.

Discover the elusive Helmeted Hornbills

There are 4 species of hornbills that are residents of the fashion Forest with the Helmeted Hornbill making the most bizarre sounds. The rest are the Rhinoceros Hornbill, the Great Hornbill and the Bushy Crested Hornbill.
