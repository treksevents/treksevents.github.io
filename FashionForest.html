Structure of the Fashion Forest

The Fashion Forest is located at the Awana Longhouse. It covers a main trail of 350m + 150m of a side trail. It offers 5 scenic bio-diversity galleries which showcases the best of an Upper Dipteropcarp Forest. Fashion Forest offers visual aids and information signs that helps guests appreciate and get to know the abundant bio-diversity of the forest.

SELF-GUIDED FASHION FOREST WALKS

Guests who signed up for this walk will be picked up from the TREKS outlet at 9.00am daily. Guest will be transferred to the entrance of the Fashion Forest located at the Awana Longhouse.

Participants will be provided with a Fashion Forest self-guided booklet / info guide. Fashion Forest guides will be at hand to provide guidance and assistance. Bottled drinking water is provided free.
