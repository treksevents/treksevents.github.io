Birding & herping

We offer 2 types of Special Interest Walks for guests

  1. Enjoy Birding
  2. Enjoy Herping

Enjoy Birding

This session is led by an experienced specialist, Mr. Henry Goh. The areas to cover is within the IBA area of Awana. The exercise will be to spot and record endemic birds and migratory birds.

`

Enjoy Herping

This session is led by an experienced specialist, Mr. Steven Wong. The areas to cover is within the Awana Eco Park. Herping is always conducted at night. Herping is an act of searching for amphibians and reptiles.
